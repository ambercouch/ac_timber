Our monthly car service plans come in three simple bundles. You can choose from 3 options and opt for the one that suits you the best. We’ve tailored these car service plans around our most requested and needed services so you can be sure you will be getting value for money and investing in the long term health of your car.

Monthly Car Service plans

Spreading the payments means you will never have to worry about not being able to afford your M.O.T and service, we’ve got it covered.

Each option is paid for monthly with a small deposit.

If you need a service more imminently you can still choose a plan. When you select your bundle you can choose Schedule B. This allows you to book your service from the day you sign up.
